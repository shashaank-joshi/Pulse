# Pulse

## Product Vision
Pulse is a personalized sports and culture intelligence platform that helps users track the teams, artists, and topics they care about, then delivers live context, recent developments, and discovery in one place.

## Problem
People follow their interests across many disconnected apps and sources. Sports updates, match schedules, music discovery, trending discussions, and interesting facts are scattered, making it hard to stay informed in one place or quickly understand what matters.

## Solution
Pulse brings together personalized interest tracking, live and upcoming sports context, discussion summaries, discovery recommendations, and interesting cultural insights into a single product experience.

## Target User
A sports and pop culture enthusiast who wants a personalized feed of updates, context, and discovery based on their interests.

## Core Product Pillars
1. Track interests
   - favorite teams
   - leagues
   - players
   - artists
   - genres
   - topics

2. Stay updated
   - live/recent scorelines
   - upcoming matches
   - recent developments around followed teams/topics

3. Understand context
   - short summaries of recent discussions/news
   - relevant facts and interesting context cards

4. Discover more
   - music and culture recommendations
   - related interests
   - lightweight interactive features like games/quizzes

## MVP
- Select and store favorite teams and artists
- Personalized homepage
- Recent and upcoming match data for followed teams
- Summary of recent news/discussion around followed teams
- Discovery cards related to followed interests

## Stretch Features
- Guess the Footballer game
- Daily briefing generation
- Interesting Now cards (sports/music/pop culture facts)
- Retrieval-based context explanations
- Recommendation ranking improvements

## Non-Goals for V1
- Full social platform
- Multi-agent architecture
- Full Spotify clone behavior
- Too many sports at once
- Complex auth and multi-user collaboration
- Real-time chat systems

## Success Criteria
- A user can select interests and get a personalized homepage
- The app shows live/recent/upcoming sports context
- The app summarizes recent developments for followed teams
- The app includes at least one discovery feature
- The system is deployed and easy to demo
- The architecture and technical tradeoffs are easy to explain in interviews
